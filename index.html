Welcome to ACTCET - Astronomy Club of TCET!

ACTCET is a diverse community, under ISRO's Outreach Program, uniting seasoned astronomers, curious beginners, and star enthusiasts. We are dedicated to making the wonders of the universe accessible to all ages and backgrounds. Our mission is to spread the joy of astronomy through carefully curated activities and events. What sets us apart is our commitment to creating a welcoming space where questions thrive, knowledge is shared, and wonder is nurtured. Join our cosmic journey, whether you're an astronomer, student, or simply curious. Welcome to ACTCET, where the fascination with the stars becomes a shared adventure for everyone.

Recent Events

Antariksh

Antariksh," organized under ISRO's Outreach Programme, is a testament to our commitment to nurturing a passion for astronomy. This initiative seeks to instill a deep appreciation for the cosmos among participants. Activities include engaging students in solar observations, exploring the life cycle of stars, and utilizing tools like Universe Sandbox for realistic simulations. Antariksh aims to create an interactive learning environment, fostering a love for astronomy through hands-on experiences. It showcases our dedication to making astronomy accessible and enjoyable, bridging the gap between theory and practice. Join us in our cosmic journey to explore, learn, and marvel at the wonders of the universe.

Across the Astroverse

In our relentless pursuit of reaching children worldwide, we birthed our podcast, "Across the Astroverse." This platform delves into a myriad of captivating topics, including Recent Space Advancements, interstellar Travel, and an array of fascinating subjects. The podcast serves as a gateway to the cosmos, aiming to ignite curiosity and passion for space exploration. As we continue our cosmic journey, anticipate a wealth of upcoming episodes exploring even more captivating aspects of the universe. Join us as we navigate the wonders of space, one podcast episode at a time, and inspire the next generation of space enthusiasts.

Zephyr Events

Zephyr in TCET is a vibrant Technicalfest, featuring a mix of innovation, learning, and excitement. Various clubs host engaging workshops and thrilling activities, inviting participants from other colleges and children. The Astronomy Club takes the spotlight, offering captivating stargazing experiences through telescopes, fostering awe and a connection with the cosmos. The event includes a Mars-themed arcade, enhancing the sense of joy and exploration. Zephyr exemplifies the Astronomy Club's commitment to making science enjoyable, blending technology and astronomy. As the night sky dazzles with celestial wonders and the Martian arcade buzzes with excitement, Zephyr becomes a celebration of knowledge and the vast universe. Join us for a cosmic adventure where learning meets pure delight.
